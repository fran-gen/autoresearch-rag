Try with your dataset

Upload two JSONL files. Each file must stay under 5 MB for this demo.

Documents

documents.jsonl

Required: document_id or id, plus body or content.

{"document_id":"policy_001","title":"Remote Work","source_type":"policy","body":"Employees may work remotely up to three days per week."}

Questions

questions.jsonl

Required: question_id, question, expected_doc_ids.

{"question_id":"q_001","question":"What does remote policy allow?","question_type":"lookup","expected_doc_ids":["policy_001"]}
Make IDs match. Every value in expected_doc_ids must exist as a document ID in the documents file. Optional helpful fields: source_type, source_types, gold_answer, answer_facts, metadata.
